Halo 3 aim and awareness

When you fire the battle rifle, be sure to aim for the head and shoot 4 consecutive shots. Don't miss or you will have to fire a fifth shot for the finishing kill.

Check your radar at all times when you are shooting another player. Your surrounding enemies will be a major part in gameplay and survival.
