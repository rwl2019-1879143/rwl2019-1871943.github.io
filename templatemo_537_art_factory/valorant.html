Valorant Ability utilization and Strategies to Win Matches

When you are on the opposing side of the map, look for openings where the enemy could surprise you. Try to aim if you are from a far distance.

Close in on your enemy if you are on the attacking side. If you are on defense, let the enemy peak and wait for your opportunity to expose them with your abilities. Use you ultimate to spot your enemy and let your teammates close on them.
